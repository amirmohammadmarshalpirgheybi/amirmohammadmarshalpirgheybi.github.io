About Me

I am a graduate researcher in Operations Research and Management Sciences at the University of Waterloo. My current work focuses on stochastic processing networks, queueing systems, and heavy-traffic approximations, with applications in healthcare operations and dynamic scheduling. I am particularly interested in developing mathematically grounded, algorithmically efficient, and data-driven decision frameworks that bridge theory and practice.
